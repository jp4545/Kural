Hign Quality Cassava Flour

HQCF is an improved cassava flour produced using a non-traditional processing technique. It is a non-fermented, white, smooth and odorless cassava flour processed from freshly harvested roots. Detoxification is primarily achieved through the grating, dewatering, and drying process and free from fermentation. This is obtained through a process of peeling, washing, grating, pressing and drying.
